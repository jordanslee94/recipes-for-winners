For when times are rough. Or if you just hate yourself.

"Jordan, you're a mad man. You've gone too far!" That's what they told me when I made this dish for my family. They must have loved it and I know you will too!

Ingredients

  • A dozen hotdogs of the lowest quality
  • 1 white onion
  • Can of tomato paste
  • 1 head of brocoli

Directions

  1. Consider skipping this dish entirely
  2. Cut onion into strips
  3. Make a "volcano" out of the hotdogs
  4. Fill the middle of the "volcano" with onions
  5. Apply tomato past over the top for some strange reason
  6. Garnish with brocoli
  7. This one doesnt even get cooked. You just eat it like this... Enjoy!
